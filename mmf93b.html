State-based functional programming

Maarten M Fokkinga
The usual ``map f'' is adapted so as to accept and produce a state value that is single-threaded through the computation. This is an elaboration of Section 4 (3 pages; not including 4.1) of the paper `` Type parametric programming with compile-time reflection'' by Tim Sheard.
