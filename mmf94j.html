A Summary of Refinement Theory

Maarten M Fokkinga
When can a program part be replaced by another? We discuss this question formally, and in doing so we summarise the work of Zwiers et al. (``A Note on Compositional Refinement'' and some private communication) and part of the work of Hoare et al. (``The weakest prespecification'' and ``Prespecification in data refinement'').
